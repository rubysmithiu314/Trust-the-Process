How to Play

You are a nonprofit director navigating the grant economy. Each turn, gain 1 Budget and draw a card. Apply to foundations for funding. Survive 30 turns. Collect programs. Highest combined score wins.

Win Condition: After 30 turns, your Final CIU Score = Budget + (Programs × 10). Compare with classmates during debrief.

Trust-Based Philanthropy: Foundation B (Oak Foundation) rewards established relationships — each approval reduces application costs and raises your approval rate up to 80%.

Approval rates based on GrantWatch (2025): foundations fund 15–30% of applicants. Established relationships improve success to 80%+ (Funding for Good, 2020).
